Our Story

The Velmora
Legacy

Velmora was born from an obsession with the invisible — the way a fragrance can define a moment, evoke a memory, and speak before words. Founded by perfumers who believe scent is the most intimate form of luxury, we craft every bottle with intention.

We source only the rarest ingredients — Bulgarian rose, Madagascan vanilla, Sudanese oud — blended by hand in small batches to ensure each fragrance is as distinctive as the person who wears it.

A Velmora fragrance is not worn. It is inhabited.
